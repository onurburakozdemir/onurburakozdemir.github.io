My Master's Thesis


MinDE protein oscillations expressed in HEK cells, with unique barcode frequencies


Creating Magnetic Spatiotemporal Signals In Mammalian Cells


MinDE is a crucial protein involved in the spatial organization of bacterial cells. It oscillates within the cytoplasm, creating a dynamic pattern that helps position the division plane during cell division. Understanding its oscillation behavior in mammalian cells provides insights into cellular organization and can have implications for studying cell division and signaling mechanisms.
