Hi! I'm Onur. I aim to bridge the gap between physics, electronics, and biology, with help of programming, machine learning, and image analysis. In my most recent research I focus on synthetic biological systems, which is in the intersection of biology and physics. Here you can explore my portfolio, including my research papers, ongoing Master's Thesis, and my goal of integrating diverse scientific disciplines.
